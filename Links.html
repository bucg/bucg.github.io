Our Sponsors

The Lansdown pub
Bristol University Conservation Group is very pleased to introduce our new sponsor...The Lansdown! After a hard day coppicing trees and building dry stone walls we like to unwind with a nice drink from our favourite pub. Essays kept you from coming on the trip and now you're missing your fellow conservationists? Then you'll find us here on Sunday afternoons. The food is fabulous (look out for our pub lunch socials coming soon) with veggie options as well. How do we find The Lansdown I hear you cry! Walk up from the union and along Clifton Road, you can't miss it. (Address: 8 Clifton Road, Bristol, BS8 1AF.)
