BUCG: Merchandise

It's the time of year where extra layers are welcome against the chilly weather so we are planning this year's fashionable BUCG clothing. We are currently deciding on whether to sell T-shirts or hoodies and what the design should be. T-shirts will cost approximately £4-£10 and about £20 for hoodies. Let us know your opinion on the T-shirt/hoodie dillema on the poll on our Facebook page or by emailing Naomi. You can sumbit your designs/ideas to Naomi as well.

N.B.: We are a non-profit group, the cost of the merchandise is how much is costs us to buy them in the first place.
